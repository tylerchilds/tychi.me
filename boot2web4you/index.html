boot2web4you: the vision

Take any device with internet connectivity and get it online, under your control in seconds.

What does that look like?

The easy way, you can walk into an Apple store or a Best Buy, point at anything and say, "I'd like Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Explorer, Lynx, Alexa, Siri, Cortana, Assistant to open https://thelanding.page or whatever landing page you prefer when you initially turn whatever the dang thing it is on.

Well how does that happen?

We build it, then buy it, then all our competitors will have to adopt our strategy. Then, that's just the way the world works. There will be no thought left of the world ever existing otherwise.

What can it do?

Anything, really.

Writing, spreadsheets, graphics, music, video, photo, orienteering, entertainment, study, therapy, business, art, friendship, romance.
